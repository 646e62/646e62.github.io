Introducing Amicus Enso

Enso is a project that applies computer science principles and information technology to legal research. In broad strokes, Enso aims to extract a quantity of publicly available data from CanLII for use in future ML/NLP and data analysis applications.

Enso is currently hosted on my Chromebook.

The Amicus project will continue to advocate for the introduction of gavels into the Canadian justice system.
